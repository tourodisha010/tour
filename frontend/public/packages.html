Bhubaneshwar · Puri · Konark

The Golden Triangle

4 Days / 3 Nights Best: Oct – Mar Easy pace

Odisha's classic circuit, done slowly. We trace the sacred trio of Bhubaneshwar's ancient temple architecture, Puri's Jagannath devotion, and Konark's sculpted Sun Temple — with time built in for markets, food and quiet mornings rather than a checklist rush.

Highlights

  • Lingaraj, Rajarani and Mukteshwar temples with a local historian guide
  • Dhauli Shanti Stupa and the Kalinga War site at sunset
  • Jagannath Temple darshan and Puri's old-town lanes
  • Konark's Sun Temple chariot wheels and Chandrabhaga beach
  • Pipili appliqué craft village en route

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive Bhubaneshwar. Afternoon temple circuit — Lingaraj, Rajarani, Mukteshwar. Evening at leisure.
  2. Day 2 Morning at Dhauli Shanti Stupa, then transfer to Puri (1.5 hrs). Jagannath Temple darshan, sunset on Puri beach.
  3. Day 3 Full day at Konark — Sun Temple, Archaeological Museum, Chandrabhaga beach. Return to Puri via Pipili village.
  4. Day 4 Leisurely morning, transfer to Bhubaneshwar for departure.

Inclusions

  • AC vehicle for all transfers and sightseeing
  • 3 nights in curated heritage/boutique stays
  • Local guide at all major temple sites
  • Daily breakfast

Asia's largest lagoon

Whispers of Chilika

2 Days / 1 Night Best: Nov – Feb (birds) Easy pace

A short, unhurried escape onto India's largest brackish-water lagoon — dolphins at dawn, migratory birds by the thousand, and fishing communities who've read this water for generations.

Highlights

  • Early-morning Irrawaddy dolphin sighting boat safari from Satapada
  • Kalijai island temple, reachable only by boat
  • Winter migratory bird colonies (Nalabana sanctuary, seasonal)
  • Breakfast with a local fishing family

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive Satapada by late morning. Afternoon boat safari — dolphins, Kalijai temple, sunset over the lagoon.
  2. Day 2 Sunrise birdwatching (seasonal), breakfast with a fishing family, departure by noon.

Inclusions

  • Private boat for all safaris
  • 1 night lagoon-view stay
  • Breakfast and one local meal

Koraput · Jeypore · Eastern Ghats

The Tribal Trail

5 Days / 4 Nights Best: Nov – Feb Moderate pace

Our deepest, slowest journey — into the Eastern Ghats to meet the Bonda, Dongria Kondh and Paraja communities on their own terms, through weekly haats, weaving villages and homestays led entirely by locals.

Highlights

  • Onkadelli's famous weekly haat, where Bonda and Gadaba traders gather
  • Dongria Kondh hill villages and their sacred Niyamgiri landscape
  • Duduma waterfall and the old colonial town of Jeypore
  • A community-run homestay evening with local storytelling

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive Koraput, orientation walk through the old town and coffee gardens.
  2. Day 2 Onkadelli weekly haat (day-specific) — meet Bonda and Gadaba traders.
  3. Day 3 Dongria Kondh villages, weaving and craft demonstrations.
  4. Day 4 Duduma waterfall, Jeypore heritage walk, community homestay evening.
  5. Day 5 Departure.

Inclusions

  • 4 nights, mix of heritage stay and community homestay
  • Local tribal-community guide throughout
  • All transfers by SUV (roads are rural)
  • Daily breakfast and dinner

Mangroves · Crocodiles

Bhitarkanika Wilderness

3 Days / 2 Nights Best: Dec – Feb Easy pace

India's second-largest mangrove ecosystem, navigated slowly by boat — saltwater crocodiles on the mudflats, over 200 bird species, and Gahirmatha's mass turtle nesting nearby.

Highlights

  • Mangrove creek boat safari through Bhitarkanika National Park
  • Saltwater crocodile spotting at Dangamal
  • Bagagahana heronry and migratory bird colonies
  • Gahirmatha beach — Olive Ridley turtle nesting site (seasonal)

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive Khola/Rajnagar gate, boat check-in, evening mangrove walk.
  2. Day 2 Full-day creek safari — crocodiles, heronry, village stop at Gupti.
  3. Day 3 Sunrise at Gahirmatha (seasonal), departure by afternoon.

Inclusions

  • Forest permits and boat safaris
  • 2 nights eco-stay near the park
  • Naturalist guide

Mayurbhanj highlands

Similipal Forest Retreat

3 Days / 2 Nights Best: Nov – Jun Moderate pace

Sal forests, waterfalls tumbling off plateau edges, and Odisha's wildest secret — the elusive melanistic tiger. Home to the Santhal and Ho tribes, whose villages border the reserve.

Highlights

  • Jeep safari to Barehipani Falls, among India's highest
  • Joranda Falls and core-zone forest drives
  • Santhal and Ho tribal village visits
  • Chahala watchtower for wildlife viewing at dusk

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive via Baripada, entry through Pithabata gate, jeep safari to Barehipani Falls.
  2. Day 2 Joranda Falls, forest core-zone drive, Santhal/Ho village visit.
  3. Day 3 Sunrise at Chahala watchtower, departure.

Inclusions

  • Forest entry permits and jeep safaris
  • 2 nights forest-edge stay
  • Naturalist-guided drives

Ratnagiri · Udayagiri · Lalitgiri

The Buddhist Diamond Triangle

2 Days / 1 Night Best: Oct – Mar Easy pace

A lesser-known chapter of Buddhism, unearthed slowly — hilltop stupas, monastic ruins and a serene relic casket, all set in the mist-fed hills near the Brahmani river.

Highlights

  • Lalitgiri's excavation site, museum and the famous relic casket
  • Udayagiri's monastery ruins and stepped tank
  • Ratnagiri's hilltop stupa complex at sunrise

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Lalitgiri excavation site and museum, Udayagiri monastery ruins in the afternoon.
  2. Day 2 Sunrise at Ratnagiri stupa complex, departure by noon.

Inclusions

Daringbadi Hill Escape

  3. Day 3 Pine grove walk, departure by afternoon.

Inclusions

Sambalpur · Hirakud · Bargarh

Sambalpuri Weave & Hirakud Trail

3 Days / 2 Nights Best: Nov – Feb Easy pace

Western Odisha's textile heartland — watch ikat come alive thread by thread on the loom in weaving villages, then chase the sunset over Hirakud, one of the world's longest earthen dams.

Highlights

  • Hirakud Dam viewpoint at sunset, over Asia's largest artificial lake
  • Barpali and Bargarh weaving villages — live ikat tie-dye and loom demonstrations
  • Samaleswari Temple in the old town of Sambalpur
  • Huma's famously leaning temple on the Mahanadi's banks

Day by day

  1. Day 1 Arrive Sambalpur, Samaleswari Temple, sunset at Hirakud Dam viewpoint.
  2. Day 2 Full day in Barpali/Bargarh weaving villages — ikat dyeing and loom demonstrations.
  3. Day 3 Huma leaning temple en route, departure.

Inclusions

  • 2 nights stay in Sambalpur
  • Weaver-led village visits
  • All local transfers
