Overview

Western Odisha's textile heartland — watch ikat come alive thread by thread on the loom in weaving villages, then chase the sunset over Hirakud, one of the world's longest earthen dams.

This itinerary is a suggested starting point — every route, stay and pace can be customized to your needs.

Highlights

Hirakud Dam viewpoint at sunset, over Asia's largest artificial lake

Barpali and Bargarh weaving villages — live ikat tie-dye and loom demonstrations

Samaleswari Temple in the old town of Sambalpur

Huma's famously leaning temple on the Mahanadi's banks
