Overview

A lesser-known chapter of Buddhism, unearthed slowly — hilltop stupas, monastic ruins and a serene relic casket, all set in the mist-fed hills near the Brahmani river.

This itinerary is a suggested starting point — every route, stay and pace can be customized to your needs.

Highlights

Lalitgiri's excavation site, museum and the famous relic casket

Udayagiri's monastery ruins and stepped tank

Ratnagiri's hilltop stupa complex at sunrise
