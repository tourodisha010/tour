Overview

Odisha's classic circuit, done slowly. We trace the sacred trio of Bhubaneshwar's ancient temple architecture, Puri's Jagannath devotion, and Konark's sculpted Sun Temple — with time built in for markets, food and quiet mornings rather than a checklist rush.

This itinerary is a suggested starting point — every route, stay and pace can be customized to your needs.

Highlights

Lingaraj, Rajarani and Mukteshwar temples with a local historian guide

Dhauli Shanti Stupa and the Kalinga War site at sunset

Jagannath Temple darshan and Puri's old-town lanes

Konark's Sun Temple chariot wheels and Chandrabhaga beach

Pipili appliqué craft village en route

Odia thali lunch at a family-run kitchen

Day by day itinerary

Day 1
Arrive Bhubaneshwar

Airport / station pickup. Afternoon temple circuit — Lingaraj, Rajarani, Mukteshwar. Evening at leisure.

Day 2
Dhauli & Puri

Morning at Dhauli Shanti Stupa, then transfer to Puri (approx. 1.5 hrs). Jagannath Temple darshan, sunset on Puri beach.

Day 3
Konark & Chandrabhaga

Full day at Konark — Sun Temple, Archaeological Museum, Chandrabhaga beach. Return to Puri via Pipili appliqué village.

Day 4
Departure

Leisurely morning, transfer to Bhubaneshwar for onward journey.
