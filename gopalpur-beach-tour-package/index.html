Overview

A once-thriving colonial-era port, now a quiet beach town where the pace slows on purpose. Long lighthouse walks, a hilltop temple, and sunsets without a crowd.

This itinerary is a suggested starting point — every route, stay and pace can be customized to your needs.

Highlights

Gopalpur lighthouse and its old port-town lanes

Taratarini Temple on twin hilltops overlooking the Rushikulya river

Sunset walks along an uncrowded beach

A local fish market and seafood breakfast
